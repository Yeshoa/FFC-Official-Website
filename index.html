Welcome to the FFC

The FFC is the governing body of football in the Forest region. It organizes events like the Forest Cup and the Forestian Champions League, promoting football and regional unity.

Why Join the FFC?

Joining is simple and takes just 30 seconds. Participate in stress-free events and watch live matches through our streams on YouTube, Twitch, and Kick!

How to Join

Fill out the form to register your team for the Forest Cup and other events. Don’t miss the chance to be part of this amazing football community!
