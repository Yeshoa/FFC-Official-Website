## One to watch

The tournament opens with a fixture that carries more weight than most first round matches. Cacusia, as host nation, has been placed in Group A and will play their opening match against Roless, the reigning Forest Cup champion. It is the kind of draw that sets a tone immediately: the side that wants to win it all against the side that already has.

Roless enter the tournament as the highest ranked nation by a considerable margin. Cacusia will be backed by their home crowd from the first whistle.

---

## A record wave of newcomers

Thirteen nations will make their Forest Cup debut in Cacusia, the largest number of debutants in a single edition. Bextoria, Oseao, Glo XI, Ecologist Hegemony, Zane Farbe Eslanudial, Bisofeyr, Azure Mines, Chaplin, Fish, Garbelia, Green Empire Karaa, Klaus Devastatorie, and Woodland Green all feature in the group stage for the first time.

Their presence speaks to the continued growth of football across Forest. Several of them have been placed in groups where a strong result is not out of the question, and at least a few will be looking to make an impression well beyond the opening round.
