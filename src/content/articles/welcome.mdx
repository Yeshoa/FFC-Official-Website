## The Forestian Football Confederation

The Forestian Football Confederation is the governing body responsible for overseeing and promoting football within Forest. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Cecims, Bilsa, the FFC was built around a simple idea: bring Forestians together through football in a way that is fun, competitive, and open to everyone.

The Confederation organizes the Forest Cup ~and the Forestian Championship League~, and manages the national teams of the region in international competitions. Beyond the pitch, it works to build a football culture that values unity and sportsmanship as much as results.

## The Website

This site is the official home of the FFC. It serves as a central place for everything related to Forestian football: a historical record of past tournaments, a space for news and updates, and a tool to keep the community organized.

Here is what you will find:

- **News**: Articles covering federation updates, tournament coverage, and football across Forest.
- **Ranking**: The FFC points table, updated each edition based on participation, roleplay, and past performance.
- **History and Statistics**: Records from past editions of the Forest Cup, including results, top scorers, and team performance over time.
