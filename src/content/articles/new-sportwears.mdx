The Forestian Football Confederation is pleased to announce the appointment of three official sportswear suppliers for the Forest Cup 2026: **Sbotjunj**, **Oberalon**, and **VGala**.

Starting this edition, all participating nations that have not submitted custom kit files will be outfitted by one of these three Forestian brands. The assignment will be handled by the FFC based on each nation's kit description and visual identity.

This agreement marks the first time the FFC has established formal partnerships with sportswear suppliers.

Among the nations that will wear these kits in Cacusia:

| Brand | Nations |
|-------|---------|
| Sbotjunj | Bilsa, Stralla, Zerphen |
| VGala | Nardin, Ruinenlust, Realizer, Bisofeyr |
| Oberalon | Lumiose, Columbiqash |

> This agreement applies to nations that leave their kit design to the FFC. Nations that submit their own FTEX kit textures are not affected.

The Confederation thanks Sbotjunj, Oberalon, and VGala for their commitment to Forestian football and looks forward to seeing their work on the pitch in Cacusia.
